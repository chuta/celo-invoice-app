## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to use cKASH?
**A:** No, it's completely optional! You can use any Celo wallet address. We just encourage cKASH to support the ecosystem.

### Q: Can I change my mind later?
**A:** Yes! You can uncheck the box anytime in Settings.

### Q: Will this affect my payments?
**A:** No, payments work the same way regardless of whether you check the box or not.

### Q: What if I don't have a cKASH wallet?
**A:** You can use any Celo wallet address. The checkbox is only for those who specifically use cKASH.

### Q: Is my data shared?
**A:** We only track whether you use cKASH for internal analytics. Your wallet address remains private.

### Q: Can I use multiple wallets?
**A:** You can only have one wallet address in your profile at a time, but you can change it anytime.
